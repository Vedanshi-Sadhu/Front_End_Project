Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is a slow cooker?
A slow cooker is a kitchen appliance designed to cook food slowly over a long period, usually several hours, to enhance flavors and tenderize ingredients.
How do I clean my slow cooker?
Most slow cookers have removable inserts that can be washed with soap and water. Ensure the electrical components are not submerged in water.

Product Questions

What size slow cooker do I need?
The size depends on your cooking needs. For a family of four, a 4-6 quart slow cooker is typically sufficient.
Can I cook frozen food in my slow cooker?
It's best to thaw food before cooking in a slow cooker to ensure even cooking and food safety.
